About Me

Hi, I’m Paolo Chaffey, a Computer Science student at Queen’s University Belfast with a passion for developing technology that enhances everyday life. With over 10 years of experience in tech—beginning with freelance projects and continuing through academic pursuits and internships—I have built a strong foundation in software development, web applications, and IoT. I’m particularly interested in creating scalable, user-friendly solutions that make a tangible impact.

I’ve had the opportunity to work as a Junior Software Developer, where I gained hands-on experience with Angular, TypeScript, and project management. Additionally, I worked as an Assistant Tutor, mentoring kids in programming, which has honed my ability to communicate complex ideas simply and effectively.
My goal is to continue expanding my knowledge and skills in areas like cloud computing, and low-level device development.
